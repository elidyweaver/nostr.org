Instead of accounts owned and controlled by a centralized company, nostr gives you ownership over your nostr through a pair of keys.


Nostr keys are long strings of letters and numbers. You can often distinguish them by their first letters: "npub" or "nsec".

  • Your nostr public key (npub) can be shared with others to find your profile or mention you.
  • Your nostr secret key (nsec) is similar to a password. You own it. No one can help you recover it, and you cannot change it. Please back it up and keep it safe. Beyond entering a nostr product, your nsec signs your content, so it can be verified as yours.

*Make sure to get a signing extension before accessing nostr on browsers.
