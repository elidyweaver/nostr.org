keys to the entire nostrverse...

Instead of accounts owned and controlled by a centralized company, nostr gives you ownership over your nostr through a pair of keys.


When you start on nostr you get a set of keys, two long strings of letters and numbers. You can often distinguish them by their first letters: "npub" or "nsec".

  • Your nostr public key (npub) can be shared with others to find your profile or mention you.
  • Your nostr secret key (nsec) is similar to a password to enter nostr products, but you own it. Please back it up and keep it safe. No one can help you recover it, and you cannot change it.

*Make sure to get a signing extension before accessing nostr on browsers.

Nostr can help prove you are real

As the use of AI spreads, nostr cryptographically signs and verifies your content, proving you posted it instead of an AI bot or an impersonator.
